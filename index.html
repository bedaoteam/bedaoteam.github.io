Bedao

Bedaoteam is a team of individuals who love competitive programming in particular and coding in general. Bedaoteam works with the desire to bring knowledge and educational values, as well as contribute to the programming environment for students in Vietnam, or more.


Bedaocontest is the result of our team's efforts to broaden the competitive programming practice environment in Vietnam by organizing monthly competitive programming contests with the support of VNOJ.
